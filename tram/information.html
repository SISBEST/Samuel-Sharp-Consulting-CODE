The S Tram

Each rail line has a color name.
All lines run daily except the Airport (Pink) Line.
Trains run about every 7 to 10 minutes, and every 10 to 15 minutes in later evenings.
There are free connections between rail lines when two stations are connected with a ——————.
At stations, a Customer Assistant is on duty to help you. Customer Assistance call buttons are located near turnstiles and on platforms.
Signs on stations indicate rail routes and destinations.
Stops and connections are announced.
